## Categories

### 1. Biological & Natural (Zoomed Out & In)
- **Botanical:** Lotus pods in a pond, dried sunflower field, protea flower, banksia seed pods, dense forest of tree sponges, cluster of mushrooms on a log.
- **Marine:** Coral reef landscape, barnacle-covered ship wreck, washed up sea sponges, fish eggs on a leaf, sea anemone colony.
- **Insects:** Massive wasp nest hanging from a tree, termite mound landscape, swarm of eggs, honeycomb frames, dragonfly eyes.
- **Fungal:** Shelf fungus on a tree trunk, puffball mushrooms, slime mold network, lichen on a rock.

### 2. Medical & Body (Human & Animal)
- **Skin Conditions:** Severe acne scarring on face, keratosis Ailaris on arm, skin graft mesh texture, healing burn marks, grafted skin, dilated pores on nose.
- **Infections:** Cluster of warts on hand, fungal infection on foot, shingles rash on back, botfly larval holes.
- **Animal:** Surinam toad (pipa pipa) back, dog paw pads, cat tongue texture, ostrich skin (leather), plucked chicken skin.

### 3. Food & Kitchen
- **Bubbly:** Boiling pancake batter, sourdough starter bubbling, aerated chocolate bar (bitten), crumpets, injera bread.
- **Fruits/Veg:** Sliced pomegranate, melon seeds, strawberry surface, cantaloupe skin, corn on the cob (missing kernels).
- **Cooking:** Boiling pasta water, foam on coffee, fermentation jar bubbles.

### 4. Everyday Objects & Materials
- **Household:** Stack of plastic pipes, bundle of straws, sponge collection, bubble wrap, hairbrush bristles.
- **Textiles:** Knitted wool blanket (chunky), crochet patterns, lace fabric, mesh sneakers, velcro hooks.
- **Construction:** Perforated metal sheet, brick wall with weeping holes, concrete air pockets, acoustic ceiling tiles.

### 5. Abstract & Surreal Landscapes
- **Geological:** Basalt columns, eroded sandstone cliffs, dried cracked earth, mud pots bubbling.
- **Surreal:** Field of eyes, wall of ears, infinite cellular structure, planet surface with craters.
